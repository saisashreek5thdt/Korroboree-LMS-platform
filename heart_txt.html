Heart – Emotion

Feel your potential

Often, as parents, we stress more on success rather than effort. In case, a child secures marks below the predefined average in an examination, we are quick to judge him as someone with a mediocre future. As soon as we tag a child as unsuccessful because of his low IQ, we immediately set a limit to his potential.

Human potential is infinite and when explored, we can turn into the best version of ourselves. This is what Korroboree believes and wants every parent, teacher, and child to strongly have faith in. You should believe in your heart that one low scoring exam is not the be-all and end-all. It is our emotional growth that helps us to keep going, even in adverse situations.

Investing in your child’s emotional and mental health goes a long way in letting them follow their heart and unleash their full potential to lead a life of happiness and abundance.
